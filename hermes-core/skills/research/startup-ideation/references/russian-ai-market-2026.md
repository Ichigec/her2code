- **20%** — companies fully replaced foreign software (K2 Cyber Security)

- **43%** — most success in software/hardware replacement

## Strategy Shift (Expert Consensus)

> "The winner will be the one who can create the most convenient, transparent, and business-effective product—one that can truly generate profit. Owning a proprietary LLM is no longer a strategic advantage." — Denis Filippov, Sputnik (Nov 2025)

> "Russian software firms should focus on building products based on generative AI and cases that bring real financial effect." — Filippov

> "Significant opportunities lie in creating applied products based on open AI models—this is where Russian companies have a chance to quickly show results."

## AI Agent Adoption in Russia (2025)

Per Softrest (Dec 2025):

- **Customer service**: auto ticket creation, status checks
- **Sales**: lead qualification, deal tracking, manager tasks
- **Legal/Admin**: contract generation, document management, version control
- **HR**: resume screening, initial interviews, onboarding
- **IT support**: infrastructure monitoring, auto-ticketing, remediation
- **Marketing**: audience analysis, content generation, media plans

Technology moved from experimental to applied in 2025. 2026 forecast: mass adoption.
